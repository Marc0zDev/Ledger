# Product

## Register
product

## Purpose
Ledger is a shared financial vault manager. Groups of people create a "cofre" (vault) with a savings goal, add participants, and track expenses together — moving toward a collective target. Primary use case: couples, families, or small groups managing a shared financial goal.

## Target Users
- Adults managing shared finances with a partner, family member, or small group
- Context: They open the app on desktop or mobile to check progress, add expenses, or invite participants
- Primary job: Know where they stand toward their goal and log what was spent

## Brand Personality
Moderno, Limpo, Confiável

## References
- Things they like: clear data hierarchy, calm dark interfaces, precise typography (think Linear, Vercel dashboard, Raycast)
- Specific: Linear's information density without clutter; Vercel's restrained dark palette

## Anti-References
- Colorful / white / Nubank-style fintech startup look
- Gamified / savings-app / infantile aesthetic
- Heavy corporate / SAP-style UI

## Accessibility
- WCAG AA minimum (4.5:1 body contrast)
- Reduced motion supported via prefers-reduced-motion

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity first** — every number and status must be instantly readable
2. **Restrained palette** — dark background, one accent color (gold), no rainbow
3. **Data over decoration** — charts and numbers lead; decoration supports, never competes
4. **Calm progression** — the UI should feel like a reliable tool, not an exciting game
